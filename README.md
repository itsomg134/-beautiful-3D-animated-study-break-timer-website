## ğŸ¯ How to Use

1. **Set Your Timer**
   - Adjust study duration (default: 25 minutes)
   - Set break duration (default: 5 minutes)

2. **Add Your Tasks**
   - Click the + button to add tasks
   - Type your task and press Enter
   - Check off tasks as you complete them

3. **Start Studying**
   - Click the Play button to begin
   - Watch the 3D animation come to life
   - Stay focused until the timer completes

4. **Take Breaks**
   - Timer automatically switches to break mode
   - Enjoy your well-deserved rest
   - Get ready for the next session!
